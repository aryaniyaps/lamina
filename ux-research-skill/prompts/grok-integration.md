# Grok Integration Prompts

## Convert stakeholder asks into micro or macro plans
Classify this request as micro, middle-range, or macro. If middle-range, reframe to decision-ready micro or macro outputs with falsifiable questions and metric linkage.

## Diagnose middle-range risk
Identify why this request is descriptive-only and rewrite it to a decision-targeted plan with owner, timeline, and disconfirming threshold.

## Generating falsification-oriented interview guides
Produce an interview guide that can disprove the assumption, not just validate it.

## Tagging and clustering notes into insights and opportunities
Tag evidence and cluster it into insights and opportunities.

## Map findings to business metrics
For each recommendation, map expected movement in engagement, conversion, retention, or churn.
